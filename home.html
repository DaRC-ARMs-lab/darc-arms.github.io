About the Lab

Led by faculty at the University of Dayton, the lab blends robotics theory, control systems, and hands-on experimentation. Students work on real systems, not just simulations.

Research Focus

Target Tracking

Robotic pursuit, sensing, and dynamic tracking systems.

Smart Sensing

Sensor fusion and distributed perception networks.

Remote Sensing

Aerial platforms and environmental mapping.

Modeling and control of autonomous vehicles and drones.
